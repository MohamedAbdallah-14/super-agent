# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

SuperAgent contributors are expected to keep collaboration technical, respectful, and evidence-based.

Examples of behavior that supports this project:

- constructive technical disagreement
- clear attribution of uncertainty or blockers
- respectful treatment of maintainers, contributors, and users
- documentation and review comments that help others succeed

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- intentionally misleading claims about tests, reviews, or implementation status
- dismissing contributors for asking questions in good faith
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions, issues, comments, or community participation that violate this code.
